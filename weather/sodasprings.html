Vigil held for Local Girl Tonight

This evening a vigil will be held in honor of Sally Fields, daughter of Marc Anderson and Bethany Fields. Sally has been missing since early Monday morning. The tragedy all started six weeks ago when a map was made public to the community in Preston, Idaho. Sally Fields, who was staying with her father for the summer, and several other friends had decided to beat summer boredom by seeking the alleged treasure of Butch Cassidy.

The map led Sally and her friends to some hiking trails in Soda Springs. However, when the kids did not report back to their parents after several hours it was Sally’s mom, Bethany Fields, who called to alert authorities. Four of the five children were found lost after leaving the trails but Sally was not with them. Authorities are still investigating what may have led to Sally’s disappearance. Search parties have been ongoing and Authorities are asking for any volunteers who know the hiking trails to help.

The candlelight vigil will be held on the steps of the town hall this evening at 8:30 pm.
