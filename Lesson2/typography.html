Typography

Headings will all use "Work Sans".

Regular paragraph text will use "Akshar".

Both fonts are imported from the google font API and deteriorate to other standard Sans-Seriff fonts. "Work Sans is bolder, heavier font with lots of curves. In contrast Akshar is a lighter font with sharper angles. The two compliment eachother by adding visual variety to the page. Headings pop a little better and the primary content stays very legible.
